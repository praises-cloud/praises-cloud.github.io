Ingredients

(Serves 6)

  • 3 large bananas
  • 115g light brown sugar
  • 205g plain flour
  • 90g unsalted butter
  • 1 large egg
  • teaspoon of salt
  • teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • teaspoon of cinnamon
  • A splash of milk
"The mosistest banana bread I have ever tasted" - Praises, 2024

Mash your 3 bananas into a bowl. Add the egg, melted butter and sugar into the bowl and whisk. Then add the remiander of the dry ingredient and mix. greese 9 inch loaf tin and pour in the batter. bake at 160 degrees celcius for 50 minutes and leave to cool
